Our team is here to assist with your delivered catering needs and to help create a meal that will be enjoyed by all.

Cal Catering will deliver anywhere on the main Berkeley campus. We will also deliver to 4th Street Shared Services Center and UCOP, with a minimum order of $250.

Before placing your order, please consider the following:

  1. Date, Time and Location your order will be needed. Same day orders may be possible if placed by 9:00 a.m.
  2. Number of people attending the event, in addition to any special dietary requirements.
  3. Chart-string for your payment. Please note, your order is not confirmed unless we have received payment information.

Once the order is placed, a confirmation email will be sent outlining the details of your order such as menu selections, date, time, and address information. Please be sure to review the information closely to ensure accuracy.
