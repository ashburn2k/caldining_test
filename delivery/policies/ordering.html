Ordering

  • Same day orders may be possible if placed by 9:00am.
  • All orders must be placed via online ordering system. You will receive a confirmation email once the order has been placed.
  • Important: If you do not receive an email confirmation of your order from us, your order has not been placed.
  • If you have a request after you submitted an order, you can submit a change/request from the email confirmation. Your changes are not confirmed until you receive a new confirmation email from us.
