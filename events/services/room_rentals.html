All individuals and groups are welcome to use the conference center and dining facilities for meeting and special functions; University affiliation is not required.

Non-Campus Users include departments, campus affiliated organizations, student groups, or other campus units which are recognized by the University.

Non-Campus Users with campus sponsorship include non-campus uses who have an established sponsorship with a campus group. Non-campus users with campus sponsorship are required to use campus sponsor for all billing of event expenses. In the event of non-payment by non-campus users with campus sponsorship, the campus sponsor will be responsible for all expenses incurred for the event. Non-campus users without campus sponsorship are welcome as well, no campus affiliation required.
