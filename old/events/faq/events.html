Events FAQ

1. What forms of payment do you accept?
Credit Card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover), Chart-String & Check
2. How late can I stay during my event?
All events held at Clark Kerr campus must conclude by 10:00 pm.
3. Can I come in the day before the event to set up?
If there are no events before your scheduled event date, you may come in and drop off materials and/or setup. If there is an event scheduled, the earliest you may come to set up is on the day of the event 1-2 hours before the event start time.
4. How do I book an event?
To book, simply contact us at 510-643-4314.  After an initial conversation to understand your needs, we will arrange a site visit, provide an initial cost estimate and begin the process of making your event a success.
5. What happens after the site visit?
After a site visit, we create a contract from the discussed details for your review.  To reserve your date, you will need to sign our Terms & Conditions and send it to us with your scheduled deposit. 
6. When do I need to commit to a date and room?
It is best to commit to a date and room as soon as you are ready to move forward
7. What are the average prices for events held at Clark Kerr Conference Center?
Pricing varies for each event depending on a number of factors including event size, space requirements and any enhancements desired.
8. What are my parking options?
Parking may be available in the Southwest Parking Lot on Warring Street. Vehicles with a Central Campus or University Annual parking permit (C or F permits) may park on site at no additional fee. If more than (10) vehicles are expected, parking options are:
  • Hire a Parking Cashier to sell parking permits at $10.00 per vehicle per day. Fee for Cashier is $25.00 per hour, with a three hour minimum. Guests will pay the cashier for their own parking permits.
  • Hire a Parking Attendant to distribute pre-paid permits at $15.00 per vehicle per day for events before 5pm, or $10.00 per vehicle per day for events after 5pm. Fee for Attendant is $25.00 per hour, with a three hour minimum. A guest list is required three (3) days prior to the event and the fees will be added to the final bill.
9. Do you provide Audio and Visual equipment and support?
Yes. We can make arrangements with ETS with all your Audio Visual needs to make your event a success. Additional costs will apply. Requests must be submitted to Catering/Events Sales Office at least thirty (30) days prior to the event. 
10. Is there WiFi connection? What is the fee?
Your Event Coordinator will be able to create a User Name and Password for your guests to log in. There is no charge for a WiFi Connection
