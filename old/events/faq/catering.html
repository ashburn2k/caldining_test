Catering FAQ

1. Do you provide catering?
Yes. Cal Catering offers a wide variety of menus and options.
2. How do I get a copy of the menu?
You may view our menus at our website http://catering.berkeley.edu/clarkkerr.html
3. What if I want to serve alcohol at my event?
An alcohol permit must be approved by the UCPD, 10 days before the event date. When alcoholic beverages are served, a non-alcoholic beverage must also be served.
4. How late in the event planning process can I change my food order?
We ask that the final menu selections be submitted (20) days before an event. Final guest counts can be submitted up to seven days. Late submissions and/or changes made after Banquet Event Order (BEO) confirmation may incur additional fees.
5. Can I bring my own catering company?
If you decide to bring an outside caterer, there is an outside caterer fee ranging from $400.00 - $1,000.00, depending the room you have your event, please contact Cal Catering for further information.
6. Can we use your kitchen, for my outside caterer to set up?
No. Your catering company must bring any equipment necessary for their preparation.
