krutch theatre

The Krutch theatre is an impressive location for a wide range of events including theatrical productions, lectures, student events, conferences, university ceremonies, and private galas. The first floor with its classic hardwood floor allows for a variety of adjustable layouts. Its second level balcony features padded seating, perfect for theatrical production seating.
