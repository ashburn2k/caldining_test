California Memorial stadium FAQ

1. How far in advance must I place my order?
We encourage you to place the order as soon as you have the date and time your order will be needed. Same day orders are possible if placed by 9:00am.
2. I have a meeting on the weekends, can I order coffee service?
Weekend deliveries are available with a minimum order of $250.00 per order. For example, if you need a breakfast order and a box lunch order, each order must be at least $250.00.
3. I would like to order a menu that is not listed on the website. How can I do that?
If you are interested in other menu options, please contact our Sales Office at 510-643-4314 to discuss with one of our experienced Event Sales Coordinators.
4. How late can you deliver?
We can deliver as late as you’d like! Deliveries after 4:00pm must meet a minimum order of $250.00.
5. I placed my order online, but I did not receive a confirmation?
Confirmations will be emailed within 24 hours of placing the order. If you do not receive a confirmation after this period, please contact the Sales Office at 510-643-4314.
6. I already placed a catering order but need to make some changes. What is the deadline to make changes?
You can make changes up to one (1) business day before your scheduled delivery.
7. I need to cancel my catering order.
To avoid a cancellation fee, the order must be cancelled by 5:00pm one (1) business day before the scheduled delivery. Same day cancellations will be charged 50% of your total order. Please contact our Sales Office at 510-643-4314 to cancel your order.
8. I’m not a staff member or student at the University, can I still place an order?
Absolutely! Please visit our ordering website to create an account.
9. How can I place a catering order?
You can start by creating an account on the ordering website. Once your account is created, you can place an order, track your previous orders and duplicate orders for future dates.
10. What is the delivery fee?
Minimum order for delivery is $100 with an additional 10% delivery fee.
11. Where do you deliver?
We deliver to the UC Berkeley campus. We also deliver to Campus Shared Services on Fourth Street and UCOP in Oakland, with a minimum order of $250.00 per delivery.
12. What types of payments are accepted?
Credit Card (Visa, Mastercard, and Discover only), check, money order, Chart-String/COAs, Intercampus Chart of Accounts.

Credit Card payments are accepted at our Cashier’s Office, located at 2610 Channing Way, 2nd floor, Berkeley, CA 94720. Check payments can be mailed to the same location.

Please note you must always have a copy of your invoice when submitting a payment.
13. In order to provide payment, my company needs a signed of a W-9 and a few other forms. Can you provide it?
Yes, please contact our Special Projects Coordinator, Chris San Diego, via email (csd@berkeley.edu) and in the subject line, please include “Payments: ” and attached any documents that need to be completed. Chris can also be reached by phone at 510-643-2198 for questions regarding payments and/or copies of paid invoices.
14. When do I need to pay for the order?
If you are paying by chart-string, payment is required during the ordering process before you submit the order. If you are paying by check or credit card, payment must be received by your delivery date.
15. Can you accommodate any dietary restrictions?
We have vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available - please visit our online menu.
